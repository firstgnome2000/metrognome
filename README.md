# metrognome

## Overview
The metrognome Addon is a powerful tool designed to help players keep track of the total gold acquired by their characters. This addon provides a user-friendly window that displays detailed information about your characters' gold accumulation.

## Features
- **Total Gold Display:** Instantly view the total gold acquired by each character.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** The addon comes with a clean and intuitive window for easy navigation.
- **Real-Time Updates:** The gold information is updated in real-time, ensuring accuracy.
- **Customization:** Tailor the addon to your preferences with customizable settings.

## Installation
1. Download the addon from [https://www.wowinterface.com].
2. Extract the files into your WoW Addons folder.
3. Launch World of Warcraft and navigate to the Addons menu.
4. Enable "metrognome" from the list of addons.

## Usage
1. In-game, type `/income` to open the metrognome window.
2. The window will display a list of your characters along with their total gold.
3. Explore additional options and settings within the addon to personalize your experience.
